Algorithm Reference Internal
ARMS uses a 16-character hex key in XXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX format. Block 1 must match a known product seed. Block 4 is a checksum — the XOR of Blocks 1, 2, and 3. Any tampering with the key invalidates the checksum and the app rejects it.
Product Seeds (B1):  A7F3 · B2E1 · C9D4 · D1A8 · E6B5 · F3C2 · 4A9E · 5D7B · 6E2F · 7C8A
B2, B3:  Random 4-char hex (0000–FFFF)
B4 (checksum):  parseInt(B1,16) XOR parseInt(B2,16) XOR parseInt(B3,16) → zero-padded to 4 hex chars
Example:  B1=A7F3 · B2=3C1A · B3=9D04 → chk=0xA7F3^0x3C1A^0x9D04=0x1CE9 → A7F3-3C1A-9D04-1CE9
